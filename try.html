MY PROJECTS

  • EdGagement Web Application

    My project is all about making teaching more engaging and rewarding for educators. I want to introduce elements of gamification into traditional teacher responsibilities so that teachers can earn points, badges, and other incentives as they complete tasks and reach goals. By doing so, I hope to motivate and energize teachers to perform at their best and achieve even better outcomes for students. But my project isn't just about adding game-like features to teaching tasks. It's also about monitoring and tracking teacher progress so that we can identify areas of strength and weakness and provide targeted support where it's needed. With ongoing evaluation and feedback, teachers will have the opportunity to improve their skills and grow as educators. Overall, I believe that gamifying teacher responsibilities and monitoring progress is a powerful way to improve teaching and learning outcomes. By giving teachers the tools and incentives they need to excel, we can create a more engaging and fulfilling experience for educators and students alike.
